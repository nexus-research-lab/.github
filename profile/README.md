> **Nexus / 道枢** is not a point tool. It is the core hub that reconnects the entire software chain around AI, while forming a shared workflow for human creativity and AI capability.  
> **Nexus / 道枢** 不是单点工具，而是以 AI 为核心，把整条软件研发链路重新连接起来，并让人类创造力与 AI 能力形成协同共生的中枢。

## Nexus Means / 「Nexus」意味着什么

- **全链路核心枢纽 / Full-chain core hub**  
  打通需求、设计、开发、测试、运维的端到端研发核心。  
  The end-to-end R&D core connecting requirements, design, development, testing, and operations.

- **人机协作共生中枢 / Human-AI symbiotic hub**  
  融合人类创造力与 AI 能力，不是替代关系，而是双向增强。  
  A shared operating model for human creativity and AI capability, built for amplification rather than replacement.

- **打破边界的关键节点 / Boundary-breaking node**  
  用一个中枢把原本割裂的工具、角色与流程重新组织起来。  
  A central node that reorganizes fragmented tools, roles, and workflows into one system.

## Research Program / 研究计划

- **AI-native software engineering**  
  探索 AI 驱动的需求理解、设计生成、代码实现、测试与运维协同。  
  Explore AI-native flows across requirements, design, implementation, testing, and operations.

- **End-to-end developer systems**  
  构建跨环节串联的工具链，而不是孤立的单点工具。  
  Build connected developer systems instead of isolated point solutions.

- **Human-AI workflow design**  
  研究人类判断、AI 执行、反馈闭环如何在真实开发环境中协同运作。  
  Study how human judgment, AI execution, and feedback loops operate together in real engineering environments.

- **Open prototypes and lab artifacts**  
  通过公开仓库持续沉淀原型、实验、工具与研究记录。  
  Publish prototypes, experiments, tools, and lab notes as open research artifacts.

## Operating Principles / 工作原则

- Treat AI as the hub, not a plug-in / 不把 AI 当插件，而把 AI 作为核心枢纽来设计系统
- Optimize the whole chain, not isolated steps / 优化整条链路，而不是单个局部环节
- Keep humans in the loop of judgment and creation / 把人的判断、创造与控制权保留在关键回路中
- Validate ideas through real tools and working systems / 用真实工具和可运行系统验证想法
